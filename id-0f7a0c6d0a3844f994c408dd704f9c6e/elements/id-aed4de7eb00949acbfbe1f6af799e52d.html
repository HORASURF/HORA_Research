Research organization ()
A research organization is an institutional entity—either a higher-education institution, a public research institute, or a dedicated research center—whose primary mission includes conducting, coordinating, or supporting research activities. It provides the structural, human, and infrastructural resources necessary for research projects and serves as the administrative and legal home for researchers and their outputs.
